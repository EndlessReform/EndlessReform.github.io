on the issues

Sustainability

A three-set plan of action

Syne is a type family designed by Lucas Descroix and Bonjour Monde for Synethesie, an art centre close to Paris. What makes Syne so interesting to meis that its five different cuts are diverse enough to create fresh and atypical associations among them. For instance, while Syne Regular is a geometric sans, it features short vertical proportions (most visible in the lowercase g above) and the Monospaced font received a special treatment to make its contours definitely unique. Open source, free for personal and commercial use.
